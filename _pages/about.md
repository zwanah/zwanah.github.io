# About Me

* I am a MSc student of Data-Driven Modeling in [HKUST](https://hkust.edu.hk/).
* My research focuses on developing robust deep learning techniques that can handle imperfect datasets, including those with bias, long-tail distributions, spurious correlations, and other forms of irregularities. I am also interested in applying mathematical optimization methods to improve the performance and computational efficiency of machine learning algorithms.
* Outside of my research, I enjoy playing basketball and have won the Chongqing Championship of the CUBA second-level league while studying at [Chongqing University](https://www.cqu.edu.cn/).
